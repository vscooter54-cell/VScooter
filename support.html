Frequently Asked Questions

Our Model Z offers the longest range at 45 miles on a single charge. Model X provides 35 miles, and Model Y offers 28 miles of range.

Charging times vary by model: Model Y takes 3.5 hours, Model X takes 4 hours, and Model Z takes 5 hours for a full charge.

All our scooters have IPX4 water resistance rating, meaning they can handle light rain and splashes but should not be submerged in water.

All Vscooter models can safely support riders up to 220 lbs (100 kg) while maintaining optimal performance.

Currently, we ship to the US, Canada, and most European countries. Contact support for specific international shipping options.
